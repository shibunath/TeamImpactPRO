Mission Statement

The Mission of the Teach India Project is to provide teachers and parents with resources, curriculum and tools to develop a global education perspective so as to deepen children's understanding of the world and its people. The Teach India Project emphasizes multicultural issues, global studies, and cultural awareness. We work with parents and teachers to prepare our children to live and work in a multicultural and global world. Our website is a repository of lessons about India and related teaching resources for teachers and parents of children K to 12. We publish an online educational Newsletter that in November 2009 had subscribers in 28 countries. Our programs include a Travel Study Fellowship through which teachers will be able to visit India.
